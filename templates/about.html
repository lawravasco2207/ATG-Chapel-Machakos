About Us

Where Faith Meets Community

At ATG Chapel Machakos, we help you grow your faith in Christ. In our family, we transform lives through salvation, enhancing the growth of the kingdom of God.

The ideal of church is described in the book of Acts 1:1-2, and like the church in Acts, ATG Chapel Machakos emphasizes worship, prayer, leadership, fellowship, ministry, evangelism, and discipleship.

Mentee Ministry

Mentee Ministries stands as a shining example of the transformative power of visionary leadership and dedicated service. Under the guidance of Bishop Lazarus Musyoka, this ministry has not only enriched the fabric of the church but has also equipped the youth with the tools to become ambassadors of the gospel.

As we reflect on the impact of Mentee Ministries, we extend our heartfelt gratitude to Bishop Lazarus for his foresight and dedication to nurture the next generation of church leaders.

Sunday School

Sunday School teachers at ATG Chapel Machakos exhibit outstanding leadership in coordinating the Melody Dancing Club. They not only educate children about the Bible but also foster creativity and teamwork through dance.

With their guidance, the Sunday school children develop their talents and express their faith through movement.

The teachers provide a supportive environment for the children to learn and grow, encouraging them to embrace their spirituality through dance. Through their leadership, the Melody Dancing Club has become an integral part of the Sunday school program, enriching the spiritual experience of the children and fostering a sense of community within the congregation.

Our Leadership

Pastors

At ATG Chapel Machakos, Pastors provide spiritual leadership, counseling and support, oversee the mentee ministry, and guide worship and teaching, greatly contributing to the congregation's spiritual growth and community engagement.

Deacons

Deacons serve various functions within Around The Globe Ministries. They assist with practical tasks like serving communion, pastoral care, organizing church events, and supporting charitable activities, contributing to the spiritual life of the congregation.

Elders

Elders in ATG Chapel Machakos provide spiritual leadership, oversee welfare, participate in decision-making, support the pastor, and foster unity within the congregation.

Church Committee

The Committee ensures the success of the church by managing events, worship services, Bible studies, and community outreach, providing invaluable service to the congregation.

How to Find Us

  • Take Mombasa Road (A109) southeast towards Machakos
  • After approximately 35 km, take the exit towards Machakos Junction.
  • Continue onto the Machakos-Kangundo Road (B62) for about 8 km.
  • As you approach Machakos town, stay on the main road.
  • Enter Machakos town and continue straight until you reach the town center.
  • Turn right onto Kenyatta Highway and continue for about 500 meters.
  • Turn left onto Ngei Rd and continue straight for about 300 meters.
  • Turn left onto Kinyua St.
  • You will see the KFA Hall on your left, opposite Equity Bank. Parking is available nearby.

  • Board a matatu or bus heading to Machakos town. The journey typically takes around 1-2 hours depending on traffic.
  • Alight at Machakos Bus Park.
  • From Machakos Bus Park, follow the walking directions:
  • Head southeast on Mwatu Wa Ngoma Rd.
  • Turn left onto Kitui Rd.
  • Continue straight for about 500 meters.
  • Turn right onto Ngei Rd.
  • Walk straight for about 300 meters.
  • Turn left onto Kinyua St.
  • Walk straight for about 100 meters, and you will see the KFA Hall on your left, opposite Equity Bank.
